> How do you get a first-time investor to trust an app with their savings?

## Context

Mahaana (YC W22) is Pakistan's first SECP-licensed digital wealth manager, founded by the team behind Sweden's Tundra Fonder. The product had to feel trustworthy enough for a first-time investor and capable enough for someone comparing funds line by line.

## My Role

I joined as Product Designer in December 2023 and became Senior Product Designer in April 2025. I lead design for the investment platform across mobile and web, working directly with engineers and the founders — no layers in between.

## What Shipped

- **Save+ pots** — goal-based saving inside the app.
- **Investment dashboards** — portfolio views built for people checking real money, not vanity charts.
- **ETF comparison tools** — side-by-side fund comparison for the decision that matters most.
- **An investment calculator in Webflow** — designed and built it; it lifted conversion 20%.
- **Figma component libraries** — shared across design and engineering, cutting design-to-dev turnaround 35%.

## Outcomes

- 10,000+ app downloads
- 4.8★+ rating on iOS, 4.0★+ on Android
- +20% conversion from the investment calculator
- 35% faster design-to-dev turnaround

## Lessons

The 35% number is the one I care about most. The fastest way to ship better product wasn't another design review — it was building the component library so engineers and I were working from the same parts. Sitting directly with engineers and founders, the gap between "designed" and "shipped" is where all the leverage is.
