# Transforming Customer Feedback Into Product Vision

## Overview

Title:
Transforming Customer Feedback Into Product Vision

Skills:
UX Strategy, Workshop Facilitation, Storyboarding

Description:
At Signal Sciences, we transformed customer advisory board feedback into a cohesive product vision through structured research and collaborative workshops. This unified teams around a user-centered growth strategy.

Impact:
Unified product teams around a shared vision driven by customer insights

---

## Details

At Signal Sciences, we needed to transform vague customer advisory board feedback into actionable product direction. This project focused on creating a coherent narrative to align technical and business stakeholders.

(( Visual: Hero image ))

---

### Making Customer Insights Actionable

Previous customer advisory board sessions produced scattered notes that weren't suitable for meaningful synthesis. I formalized our approach to capturing qualitative insights during the two-day event, ensuring we gathered structured feedback that could drive product decisions.

I stocked the room with workshop materials and facilitated focused discussion sessions, guiding participants to share concrete examples and specific challenges.

(( Visual: notes on the wall photo ))

---

### Synthesizing Themes as a Team

Following the event, I led a collaborative synthesis session with the entire product organization. Through structured exercises and discussion, we transformed raw feedback into clear themes and opportunities.

A key insight emerged around leveraging shared, community intelligence to improve our customers' security workflows. The team recognized this could also create compelling differentiation for customer acquisition.

(( Visual: group affinity mapping photo ))

---

### Building the Vision Together

To maintain momentum, I organized and facilitated a 1.5-day workshop to develop this community-centric security intelligence concept. I owned the full process—from creating the facilitation structure and materials to handling participant recruitment and logistics.

I brought together product managers, engineers, and designers to collaboratively explore how this vision could come to life.

(( Visual: team slide ))

---

### Finding Key Moments in the Journey

The early workshop sessions focused on understanding our current customer journey and identifying specific moments where community intelligence could have the greatest impact.

We mapped out customer workflows and highlighted three key moments where we thought we could make the biggest difference.

(( Visual: journey on the whiteboard photo ))

---

### From Concepts to Story

I guided the team through exercises to develop concepts addressing these key journey moments. We concluded by creating rough storyboards that showed how these ideas could work together as a cohesive experience.

(( Visual: workshop photos ))

---

### Crafting the Narrative

After the workshop, I collaborated with product and design leadership to refine our concepts into two clear narrative storyboards:

1. A pre-sales discovery experience
2. An enhanced setup process with our sales team

(( Visual: carousel of storyboard steps ))

---

### Impact: A Clear Path Forward

This collaborative approach transformed scattered customer feedback into a unified product vision that:

- Gave teams clear direction for improving customer experience
- Created shared language for discussing product capabilities
- Informed the product roadmap based on the most meaningful customer feedback

Through structured facilitation, careful synthesis, and clear storytelling, we replaced informal direction with an informed vision that teams could act on with confidence.
